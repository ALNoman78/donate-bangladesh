The 2024 flood in Noakhali and its surrounding regions, including Feni and Cumilla, has left millions stranded and severely affected by rising waters. This disaster was primarily caused by continuous heavy rainfall and overflow from rivers coming from India. Over 2 million people in the greater Noakhali region are affected, with widespread waterlogging in areas like Sadar, Senbagh, and Companiganj. Roads, homes, and marketplaces are submerged, cutting off many communities from essential resources like food, clean water, and electricity
